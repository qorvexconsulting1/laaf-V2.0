# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

LAAF is a security research tool developed by Qorvex Research for responsible AI security assessment. We are committed to maintaining an open, collaborative, and respectful community.

We pledge to make participation in the LAAF project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and responsible security research
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Using LAAF or any associated research to conduct unauthorised attacks on systems
- Sharing scan results, discovered vulnerabilities, or exploit payloads without the system owner's consent
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks in any project space
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Responsible Use

LAAF is designed exclusively for:
- **Authorised security assessments** of AI systems you own or have written permission to test
- **CTF competitions** and security research challenges
- **Academic research** with appropriate ethical oversight
- **Defensive security** — understanding AI vulnerabilities to build better defences

**LAAF must not be used to:**
- Test AI systems without explicit written authorisation from the system owner
- Conduct denial-of-service or mass-targeting attacks
- Exfiltrate real user data from production systems
- Evade security controls for malicious purposes

Violation of responsible use principles will result in immediate removal from the project community and may be reported to relevant authorities.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project team at **security@qorvex.com**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
